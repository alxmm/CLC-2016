AUTHOR'S INSTRUCTIONS:

Papers are invited on Constructal Law and the Second Law (irreversibility, entropy generation, exergy destruction). Abstracts of no more than 300 words should be submitted. Abstracts should clearly state the purpose, results and conclusions of the work to be described in the final paper. Final acceptance will be based on the full-length paper.

The language of the conference is English

Authors must have registered at the deadline of paper submission (14 April 2017), otherwise their paper will not be included in the proceedings.
In addition, the papers of authors who would not show up cannot be selected for publication in the special issue of the Proceedings of the Romanian Academy.
